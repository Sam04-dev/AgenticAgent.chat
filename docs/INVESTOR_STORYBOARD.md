# Investor Video Storyboard: The AGENTICAGENT.CHAT Shield

**Theme:** Institutional Trust through Transparency and Fail-Closed Security.
**Visual Style:** Institutional Light (Clean, Professional, Data-Rich).

---

## Scene 1: The Vision
*   **Narrative:** "In the high-stakes world of autonomous DeFi, trust isn't earned—it's engineered. Welcome to AGENTICAGENT.CHAT."
*   **UI Action:** Start on the landing page or onboarding screen. Show the background-removed logo prominently.
*   **Highlight:** Logo glow and crisp typography.

## Scene 2: Human-Centric Identity
*   **Narrative:** "It starts with identity. Choose a sentinel personality that matches your risk appetite: Guardian for stability, Scout for exploration, or Predator for aggressive alpha."
*   **UI Action:** Navigate through the Onboarding wizard.
    *   Click 'Guardian'. Show the Diamond NFT pulse.
    *   Click 'Scout'. Show the Triangle NFT pulse.
    *   Enter a name (e.g., "Sentinel-01").
*   **Highlight:** Personality selector and SVG Identity NFT rendering.

## Scene 3: The Risk Terminal (Institutional Light)
*   **Narrative:** "Your command center. A professional-grade terminal designed for the next era of finance. By default, it operates in Institutional Light mode—clean, airy, and focused on clarity."
*   **UI Action:** Landing on the main Dashboard.
    *   Toggle Light/Dark mode once to show versatility.
    *   Toggle it back to Light mode.
*   **Highlight:** Mode switch icon (Sun/Moon) and glassmorphism transitions.

## Scene 4: The Fail-Closed Master Switch
*   **Narrative:** "Complete control at your fingertips. The AI Automation Master Switch allows you to instantly pause all autonomous execution, while our fail-closed logic ensures the system halts safely on any critical violation."
*   **UI Action:**
    *   Toggle 'AI Automation' OFF (Show 'System Paused' notification).
    *   Toggle 'AI Automation' ON.
*   **Highlight:** Master Switch UI and real-time status updates.

## Scene 5: AI Transparency (Deep-Dive)
*   **Narrative:** "Zero black boxes. With our reasoning deep-dive, every trade is backed by a verifiable chain of thought, signed with EIP-712 integrity."
*   **UI Action:**
    *   Click on a live trade or the 'Deep-Dive' button in the Terminal.
    *   Scroll through the reasoning steps (Chain of Thought).
*   **Highlight:** The 'Deep-Dive' modal and 'EIP-712 Signed' badge.

## Scene 6: Strategic Calibration
*   **Narrative:** "Fine-tune the brain. Adjust Sentiment weights or Liquidity floors in real-time to adapt to shifting market conditions."
*   **UI Action:** Switch to the **Operations** tab.
    *   Drag the 'Sentiment Weight' slider from 0.5 to 0.7.
    *   Drag the 'Liquidity Floor' slider.
*   **Highlight:** Interactive sliders and real-time socket feedback.

## Scene 7: Scaling the Fleet
*   **Narrative:** "Manage a global fleet of agents. Track performance, uptime, and automated payroll via the ARC Layer 1 protocol."
*   **UI Action:** Switch to the **Fleet** tab.
    *   Highlight the 'Active Agents' count.
    *   Show the 'Payroll & ARC' section with pending invoices.
*   **Highlight:** Fleet status dashboard and payroll integration.

## Scene 8: The Conclusion
*   **Narrative:** "AGENTICAGENT.CHAT. The Shield of the Autonomous Economy."
*   **UI Action:** Zoom out to show the full dashboard one last time.
*   **Highlight:** System Live badge and 'Professional Risk Terminal' tagline.
