# Privacy Policy - AGENTICAGENT.CHAT

## 1. Data Collection
AGENTICAGENT.CHAT is a self-hosted application. It does not collect or transmit your private keys or personal data to any central server.

## 2. On-Chain Data
Task Intents and authorizations are submitted to the blockchain (e.g., Sepolia). This data is public by nature of the blockchain.

## 3. Logs
Logs are stored locally on your machine. You are responsible for their security and disposal.

## 4. Third-Party APIs
The software interacts with third-party APIs (Kraken, Groq, Infura). Please refer to their respective privacy policies.
